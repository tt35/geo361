UN Cities Poulation Rankings from 1950-2030


This map shows the cities with the largest population

First, click a window tab next to "UN City Rankings" and choose a year you want to see.

  • Big circles mean the cities have large population.
  • "Ranking order" shows numeric ranking order.
  • You can click each dot to see the detail of the city.

Source: United Nation Department of Economics and Social Affairs
